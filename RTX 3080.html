INNOVATIVE DESIGN

New dual axial flow through thermal design delivers the highest possible thermal performance without compromising fan acoustics. The flow through thermal design reinforces the natural air flow in the chassis for a quieter and cooler gaming system.
NVIDIA’s Ampere architecture delivers the highest performance and performance per watt.
GDDR6X, the world’s fastest graphics memory, enables faster framerates.
New HDMI 2.1 port
18 power supply phases deliver power efficiently, with headroom for overclocking.
PCIe Gen 4 allows more bandwidth for detailed textures to build more complex worlds.
The front fan pushes hot air out extra-large bracket vents. Individually shaped fins direct airflow for better cooling.
The revolutionary back-side fan draws air through the card toward the natural top and rear chassis exhausts.
The hybrid vapor chamber includes a heat pipe heat-sink that efficiently distributes heat and allows air to flow through.
The short PCB and high-density layout are enabled by a new 12-pin power connector, allowing more space for airflow through the cooling fins.
The exposed fin-stack visually elevates and functionality enhances the airflow-centric design. Together with the unibody frame, this offers maximum rigidity and fin-stack volume with minimum air resistance.
